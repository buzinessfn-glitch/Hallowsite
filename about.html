About Hallow

Founded in 2026. Built on discipline, teamwork, and the relentless pursuit of victory.

Who We Are

Built to Compete.

Hallow is a competitive esports organisation built from scratch in 2026. We don't believe in middle ground — you're either committed or you're not. Our teams compete across Valorant, Fortnite, Rainbow Six Siege, and CS2 with a singular goal: winning.

Every player under the Hallow banner represents more than just a team. They represent a standard — of preparation, mental readiness, and teamwork that doesn't break under pressure. We invest in our athletes so they can invest in results.
